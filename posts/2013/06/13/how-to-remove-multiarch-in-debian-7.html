How to remove multiarch in debian 7

2013-06-13 09:04
    Tags : 
  • debian
  • linux
  • multiarch

Just a quick one, for me to remember and for you to enjoy.

dpkg -l | grep :i386 | cut -s -d ' ' -f3 | xargs apt-get remove -y
dpkg --remove-architecture i386
apt-get update
